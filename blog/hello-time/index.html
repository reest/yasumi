Coming April will be 3 years since Yasumi saw its first daylight! In these 3 years I've worked on Yasumi mostly in my spare time and can tell you that quite some hours have been consumed.

But it was not only me of course: many contributors have helped to improve, expand and fix Yasumi in the course of these three years. A few fun facts:


Supported Countries/Regions

98

Downloads

76K

Releases

9

Contributors

30


These numbers are a mere fraction of other, larger Open Source initiatives, however I truly never thought Yasumi would be downloaded over 76K times. Long live OSS !

This first blog post is the first in - hopefully - many to keep you up to date in a different way about the progress of Yasumi. Most communication and interaction takes primarily place on Github mostly about issues and PR's. With this new blog, I'm hoping to share in a different manner information about releases and future features of Yasumi.

v2.0 - PHP7

Heads up! v1.8.0 will be the likely the last release to support PHP5.x. PHP7 has been out for a long time and Yasumi can definitely benefit from the new features in PHP7.

As always a big thanks to all the contributors and don't hesitate to log any issues, or even better submit PR's!

Cheers!
